    On the following pages you will find my pictorial family tree, links to my various genealogies and links to other sites about the family names I am researching. 
    I have posted the 1855 census for Troupsburg, Steuben County, New York.  I went to the County Clerks office in Bath, NY and hand-copied the entire census and put it on my computer and posted here for all to use. I uploaded my  newest version on 2/13/99, with spelling corrections.
I have also posted the early births for Troupsburg, New York. They run from 1847 to 1900. 
Most of the Troupsburg information has been transferred to my Toupsburg web page located at http://freepages.genealogy.rootsweb.com/~troupsburg
I have also added to my pages here a list of Steuben County CEMETERY LOCATIONS. Click on the words underlined to go to that list.
The Painted Hills Genealogical Society has added a cemetery index list to their site and if you are looking for someone you will want to check this out.
